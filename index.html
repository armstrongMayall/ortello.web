The Solution

Ortello.ai gives you governance and visibility across the Agentic Layer.

It sits between your data and the AI systems that use it, defining what can be shared, how it's accessed, and under what terms.

What it does:

  • Connects your enterprise systems via secure APIs
  • Sets and audits data permissions across MCP, ACP, and related protocols
  • Monitors agentic visibility, engagement, and conversion
  • Reports on how your brand is represented and discovered in AI environments
